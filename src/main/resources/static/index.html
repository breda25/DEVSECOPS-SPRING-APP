2026 Iran War

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia (Archived 2026)

On 28 February 2026, a joint military operation codenamed Epic Fury (US) and Roaring Lion (Israel) initiated a series of decapitation strikes across Tehran.

Conflict Profile

Date: Feb 28, 2026

Status: Ongoing

Strait of Hormuz: Closed

Location: Middle East, Cyprus, Caucasus

Retaliation Phase

Iran responded with Operation True Promise IV, launching over 285 ballistic missiles and 1,500+ drones targeting Israeli infrastructure and US regional bases in Bahrain and Kuwait.
